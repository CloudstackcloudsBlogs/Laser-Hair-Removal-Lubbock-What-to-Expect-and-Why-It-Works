The Process: What to Expect Before, During, and After Treatment

Laser hair removal is straightforward, but we know that undergoing any aesthetic procedure for the first time can come with some uncertainty. Here’s what the experience typically looks like at our clinic:

Visit Us

Before Your Session

We recommend avoiding sun exposure and waxing for at least two weeks prior to treatment. You’ll need to shave the area being treated the day before or the morning of your appointment so that the laser can focus its energy on the follicle beneath the skin, not the hair above it.

Wear comfortable clothing and avoid applying lotion, deodorant, or cosmetics to the treatment area.

During the Session

Most appointments last between 15 minutes to an hour, depending on the size of the area being treated. The sensation is often compared to a light snap from a rubber band. While it’s not completely painless, many clients find the process much more tolerable than waxing.

We use cooling techniques and devices to make the treatment as comfortable as possible. Our staff will talk you through each step, ensuring you’re never caught off guard.

Aftercare and Follow-Up

You may notice some redness or mild swelling shortly after the procedure, which typically fades within a few hours. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are always available to answer questions following your visit.

Hair will gradually fall out over a period of days to weeks. Multiple sessions (typically 6 to 8) are necessary to catch all the hair in its active growth phase. Once your series is complete, you can expect significantly reduced regrowth and softer, finer hair.
